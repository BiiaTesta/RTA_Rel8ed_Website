RTA Captain Duties



As RTA Captains, they will be responsible for:

  • Serving as Points of Contact: Acting as the main point of communication between students, instructors, and staff.
  • Supporting New Members: Providing guidance to new students and helping them navigate the RTA resources.
  • Coordinating Events and Sessions: Assisting in the planning and execution of RTA meetings, workshops, and the Hackathon.
  • Facilitating Collaboration: Encouraging teamwork and fostering a supportive learning environment.
  • Sharing Insights and Feedback: Offering input to continuously improve the RTA experience for everyone.
  • Leading Physical Events: Heading RTA events at Brock University, UNLV, and other partner sites in their respective regions.
  • Hosting Friday Fun Games: Taking charge of our weekly RTA Friday Fun Games to create a space for networking, fun, and engagement!
